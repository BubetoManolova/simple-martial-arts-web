Martial arts as you may know are many and an extremely diverse. Today these arts are available for studing almost anywhere in the world. If you are interested in martial arts first you have to decide what would be better for you and then where to train. It would be very difficult to look at history and philosophy of absolutely all of the known types of martial arts, which is why here we will provide you information for the most popular, famous or in other words, the most widespread martial arts.
History of martial arts as a whole starts from ancient times. It is believed that the beggining starts with the eastern martial arts. Like any other history there are many contradictions for the exact founders and creators.
In fact we can say that the roots of martial arts are hidden in the ancient times. But where exactly, might not be able to clarify never. According to writings of some legends about 2,000 years ago in India has formed technique to combat, which gave the foundations of martial arts. According to other historical information the beggining starts in the Shaolin monastery when a teacher called Bodhiharma arrived. He was born in India, from a rich family. He left home and started rushing around southern parts of Asia. After a time reached the Shaolin monastery in northern China, where impose their methods. The idea was to give the students a system for health and longevity, which added prolonged meditation in Buddhist monasteries.
It is important to know that each style has it's own creator, but as we already mentioned, now we look at the history of martial arts as a whole - in infancy.
